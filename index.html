How It Works

Connect once. Chat forever.

No new software to learn. No training. ShelfAI connects to your existing system and works through a simple chat — the same way you already message your team.

1

Connect to What You Already Have

We connect ShelfAI to your existing Google Sheet, Airtable, Notion database, or any spreadsheet you already use. Nothing changes about how your data is stored — we just add a layer on top.

  • Works with Google Sheets, Airtable, Notion, Excel
  • Your data stays exactly where it is
  • No migration, no data entry, no disruption
  • Set up in 48 hours — we handle everything
You do not change your system. ShelfAI learns your system.
2

Ask Questions, Make Updates — By Chat

Once connected, you manage inventory entirely through conversation. Ask what is low. Say what you just received. Tell it what was used. ShelfAI updates your inventory in real time.

  • "What's running low?" — instant answer from your live data
  • "Got 6 cases of oat milk today" — updates automatically
  • "We used 3kg of flour this morning" — deducted instantly
  • "Add hand sanitiser, 20 bottles, minimum 5" — new item added
The bot always confirms before making any change. Nothing updates without your approval.
3

Get Alerts Before Problems Happen

Every evening ShelfAI checks your inventory automatically and sends you a summary. You see what needs ordering before the next morning — not after you run out.

  • Daily stock alert — sent automatically every evening
  • Instant alert when any item drops below your minimum
  • Clean summary — only what needs your attention
You know about the problem the evening before. Not during the morning rush.
4

Your Existing System Stays Clean and Up to Date

Every update made through the chat reflects instantly in your original spreadsheet or database. Your team can still access it the way they always have — ShelfAI just keeps it accurate.
